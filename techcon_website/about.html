History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has since grown into a global technology conference attracting thousands of professionals each year.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, diversity, and collaboration in the tech community. We aim to inspire the next generation of creators and leaders through meaningful conversations and experiences.
